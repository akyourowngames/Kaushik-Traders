ABOUT KAUSHIK HARDWARE

Established in 1998, Kaushik Hardware started as a small family-run shop in the heart of the city. Over the last two decades, we have grown into one of the most trusted suppliers of industrial tools and building materials in the region.

We believe in honest pricing and genuine advice. Whether you are a large-scale contractor building a high-rise or a homeowner fixing a leaky tap, we treat every customer with the same respect and dedication. Our goal is simple: to help you build stronger.
